    The Start of My BYU-Idaho Journey

    Beginning my journey as a Software Development student at BYU-Idaho has been nothing short of inspiring. I’m eager to explore programming, software design, and database management while shaping my future in tech.

    Why BYU-Idaho?

    BYU-Idaho stood out because of its emphasis on hands-on learning and values-driven education. The flexibility to study remotely has allowed me to manage my time effectively while pursuing both academic and personal growth.

    As someone passionate about technology, I felt this program would give me the foundation I need to excel in a software engineering career. The resources and supportive community have made this experience truly enriching.

    My approach to learning

    Learning at BYU-Idaho has taught me the importance of consistent effort. I dedicate time daily to understanding concepts, practicing coding exercises, and collaborating with my peers on projects.

    I’m also building a portfolio that reflects my skills and interests, which includes projects like a car sales platform (*NextRide*) and a business directory for small-scale enterprises. These projects challenge me to think critically and develop innovative solutions.
