    Volunteering and Tech: A Balancing Act

    Juggling my passion for technology with a commitment to serving others has taught me invaluable lessons about time management, empathy, and the power of small acts of service.

    Why volunteering matters

    My experience as a full-time missionary in the Nigeria Uyo Mission profoundly shaped how I approach life and work. Serving in Akwa Ibom and Cross River States instilled a deep sense of responsibility and the desire to contribute positively to my community.

    Even while pursuing a demanding academic and career path, I strive to maintain a spirit of service. It reminds me that technology is not just about innovation but also about creating solutions that improve lives.

    Finding balance

    Balancing volunteering with academic and professional responsibilities requires intentionality. I plan my week carefully, ensuring that I dedicate time to community service without compromising my commitments to learning and work.
